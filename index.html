ABOUT US

Polgi Games is an independent game studio based in Cologne and Istanbul, founded with a simple goal: to create charming, meaningful, and memorable gaming experiences.
Polgi Games is small in size, big in dreams and always open to feedback, ideas, and collaboration.

OUR GAMES

We create playful, heartfelt, and immersive experiences that spark curiosity and joy. Each of our games is crafted with care from the tiniest interaction to the overall game world to deliver something that feels cozy, polished, and uniquely ours.
